ABOUT US


YBIKE is a motorcycle dealer formed by Calvin Joestar on December 30, 2016 and based in Jakarta, Indonesia. YBIKE sells motorcycles from 2 famous brands of motorcycle: Vixian and Kawasako. All of our products are kept inside a secure warehouse and covered with a large fabric to keep them clean of dusts.
